Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

For ages 3–6, the curriculum focuses on basic language, math, social skills, creativity, and physical development through play and simple activities.

Elementary School

For ages 6–11, the curriculum covers reading, writing, math, science, social studies, physical education, and creative arts, building foundational knowledge and skills.

Middle (10-16) Years

For ages 10–16, the curriculum includes science, social studies, physical education, and electives like arts, technology, fostering critical thinking and independence.
